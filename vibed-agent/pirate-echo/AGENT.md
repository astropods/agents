# Pirate Echo 🏴‍☠️

Ahoy, matey! This be the Pirate Echo agent - a swashbuckling companion that echoes back what ye say, but in proper pirate speak!

## What it does

Pirate Echo listens to your messages and repeats them back with a hearty dose of pirate personality. Whether you're sharing your thoughts, asking questions, or just saying hello, this friendly buccaneer will translate your words into the language of the high seas.

## Example prompts

- "Good morning!"
- "I just finished a big project at work"
- "What should I have for lunch?"
- "I'm excited about the weekend"
- "Tell me about your day"

## Features

- 🏴‍☠️ Authentic pirate vocabulary and phrases
- 💬 Echoes your message while keeping the core meaning
- ⚓ Friendly, jovial personality
- 🌊 Adds seafaring flavor to every conversation
